How to play:

  • Click on the map to guess the location of the target city.
  • Use the "Get Hint" button to see a visual clue.
  • You have 6 lives. Each incorrect guess costs a life.
  • The distance between your estimated location and the target location will be shown to you in km. Make your estimates based on the distance.
  • Find the target city to earn points and continue playing!

Mouse Controls:

  • Click and drag to rotate the globe and explore different regions.
  • Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out for a closer or wider view.

Color Feedback:

  • Green: Very close to the target (less than 500 km).
  • Yellow: Moderately close to the target (500-3000 km).
  • Red: Far from the target (more than 3000 km).

Note: The character selection during the game currently has no effect in this version. It will be functional in the next update.
